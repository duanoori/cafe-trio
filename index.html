Our Services


At Cafe Trio, we offer a wide range of services to satisfy your coffee cravings and dining needs. From expertly crafted beverages to delicious homemade pastries, we have something for everyone.

Our services include specialty coffee brewing, afternoon tea service, private events hosting, and barista training workshops.



About Our Cafe


Welcome to Cafe Trio, where we serve the finest coffee and homemade pastries in town. Our cafe was established in 2010 with a passion for quality ingredients and warm hospitality.

We source our coffee beans directly from ethical growers and roast them in-house to ensure maximum freshness and flavor.
